Mojito Recipe

A renowned favourite, this drink can be altered to suit your taste. Ingredients you will need are:


  • 50ml White Rum
  • 15ml Gomme
  • Half of a lime
  • Mint Leaves
  • 100ml Soda Water


STEP 1

STEP 2

STEP 3

Top up with soda water, garnish with mint and serve!
