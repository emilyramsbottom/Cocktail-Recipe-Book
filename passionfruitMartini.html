Passionfruit Martini Recipe

To ensure you get that boost of fruity flavour, you will need the following ingredients:


    • 50ml Vodka
    • 25ml Passionfruit Syrup
    • 75ml Pineapple Juice
    • 5ml Vanilla Syrup
    • 5ml Lemon Juice
    • Half a Passionfruit to Garnish


STEP 1

Scoop the seeds from one of the passion fruits into the glass of a cocktail shaker. Add the vodka, passoa, lime juice and sugar syrup. Add a handful of ice and shake well.

STEP 2

Strain into 2 martini glasses, top up with prosecco, then add half a passion fruit to each.

STEP 3

Serve up and enjoy!
