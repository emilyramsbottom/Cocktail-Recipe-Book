Passionfruit Martini Recipe

  • 50ml Vodka
  • 25ml Passionfruit Syrup
  • 75ml Pineapple Juice
  • 5ml Vanilla Syrup
  • 5ml Lemon Juice
  • Half a Passionfruit to Garnish
Shake and Strain into Martini Glass. Garnish with Passionfruit.
