Espresso Martini Recipe

Coffee lovers - this one is for you! If you want a drink with a buzz, ingredients are below:


  • 25ml Vodka
  • 25ml Kahlua
  • 50ml Espresso
  • 5ml Vanilla Syrup
  • 3 coffee beans to garnish


STEP 1

Add vodka, kahlua, espresso and syrup to a shaker filled with ice and shake until well-chilled.

STEP 2

Strain the mixture into a chilled cocktail glass.

STEP 3

Garnish with your coffee beans, and serve up immediately!
